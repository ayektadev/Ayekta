Mission

“Health in Oneness.” Ayekta advances digital equity by bringing reliable, traceable record‑keeping to the last mile of care. We pair a lightweight EMR with consulting to adapt rollouts to real‑world constraints.

Why Ayekta?

More than software — field‑first design plus on‑the‑ground support.

Offline → Cloud Sync

Local capture with encrypted sync when connectivity exists. Zero lock‑in exports.

Role‑specific Workflows

Doctor, Pharmacist, Lab Tech flows to speed throughput and reduce errors.

Append‑only & Auditable

Every encounter versions a bundle for accountability and continuity of care.

Lightweight by Design

Ergonomics over feature bloat. Stability in low‑resource conditions comes first.
